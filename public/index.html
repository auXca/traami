Why Traami is different

🔁

Rota-Aware Matching

No other carpool app understands shift work. Set your rota and we match you with colleagues on the same schedule.

🏥

Workplace Carpooling

Connect with people from the same hospital, warehouse or office going the same direction, NHS, Amazon, factories and more.

📍

Route Corridor Matching

We find rides passing near you along the same route, not just exact matches. Like BlaBlaCar but built for shift workers.

💷

Fair Fuel-Based Pricing

Prices are calculated from actual fuel costs. Drivers earn back their fuel money, passengers save on every commute.

⭐

Driver Ratings

Every ride is rated. You always know who you're riding with before you request a seat.

💬

In-App Chat

Message your driver directly inside the app. No need to exchange phone numbers.

🤝

Quick Match

Met a colleague at work? Search them by username or phone, check if your route passes near their home or a bus stop, and add them instantly.
