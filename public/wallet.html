💷 How You Get Paid

1. Passenger pays Traami — After your ride is approved, the passenger sends payment to Traami's Revolut or Monzo.
2. Traami credits your wallet — 90% of the ride fare is added to your balance automatically when you mark the ride complete.
3. You withdraw anytime — Request a withdrawal below. We'll send it to your Revolut, Monzo or bank account within 24 hours.

Traami keeps: 10% platform fee  |  You keep: 90% of every ride

Once you've completed a ride, mark it here to get your earnings credited to your wallet. Only mark it complete after the passenger has been dropped off.
